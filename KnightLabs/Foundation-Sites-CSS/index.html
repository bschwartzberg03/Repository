Career of Chris Davis

Chris Davis played a 13-year career in Major League Baseball. The one-time All-Star played his first three seasons in Texas as a Ranger before being traded in the 2011 season to the Baltimore Orioles. Davis finished his career in Baltimore, making his lone All-Star game in 2013. He slugged a career-best 53 home runs to go alongside a career-best 138 RBIs, a career-best 1.004 OPS, and a career-best third-place American League Most Valuable Player finish. But Davis struggled over the final few years of his career, finishing with a sub-.200 batting average in each of his final three seasons.
